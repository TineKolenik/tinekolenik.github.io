Biography

Dr. Tine Kolenik is a researcher, scientist, lecturer, assistant editor and data science & AI engineer primarily working in the fields of cognitive science, mental health, and sustainability. He is currently affiliated with LoginEKO, The Institute of Synergetics and Psychotherapy Research of Paracelsus Medical University, and Informatica (an international scientific journal).
